The Story

Lynn and Jane are like a lot of teen girls their age: they are doing their best at school, they love listening to music, and they spend time connecting with friends and family on social media. Lynn expresses her artistic talents with a deep devotion to calligraphy, while Jane is a highly talented visual artist who excels in soft pencil sketching. Both young ladies are also devoted anime and manga enthusiasts, just like their father. a parent, Kenneth felt it was his responsibility to directly enable access for his daughters to contribute and build a better world, one where they are empowered to make their own free choices. As Kenneth watched his daughters grow up and mature, there was sincere concern about the world they were entering. The world just did not seem as safe as his own late 20th century childhood. It is certainly not as innocent and equitable as most socially conscious parents would like it to be. True gender equality, where men and women have equal power and identical opportunities for financial independence, education, and personal development should be basic universal rights. The new world opened by NFTs and crypto philanthropy is one where girls can grow up and become women who are recognized and respected for who they are, for what they have already accomplished, for their commitment to their aspirations and future endeavours, and to feel empowered to speak out on the issues for which they have strong convictions.
